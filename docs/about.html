About ThreatForce

Empowering SMEs to defend against cyber threats through human-focused security training.

Problem Statement

Many SMEs operate with limited cybersecurity infrastructure and lack dedicated personnel. Employees often unknowingly click phishing links or reuse weak passwords. ThreatForce focuses on the human side of cybersecurity to mitigate risk.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to equip SMEs with interactive, scenario-based training. We help employees recognize phishing, maintain password hygiene, respond to ransomware threats, and adopt industry best practices.
